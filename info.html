Short View

Chennai is ranked as a beta-level city in the Global Cities Index,[33] and was ranked the best city in India by India Today in the 2014 annual Indian city survey.[34][35] In 2015 Chennai was named the "hottest" city (city worth visiting and worth living in for long term) by the BBC, citing the mixture of both modern and traditional values.[36] National Geographic mentioned Chennai as the only South Asian city to feature in its 2015 "Top 10 food cities" list.[37] Chennai was also named the ninth-best cosmopolitan city in the world by Lonely Planet.[38] In October 2017, Chennai was added to the UNESCO Creative Cities Network (UCCN) list for its rich musical tradition.[39] More than one-third of India's automobile industry being based in the city. Home to the Tamil film industry, Chennai is also known as a major film production centre. It is one of the 100 Indian cities to be developed as a smart city under the Smart Cities Mission.[40]
